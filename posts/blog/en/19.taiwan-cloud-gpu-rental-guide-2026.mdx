## Payment and Invoicing: USD, Fees, and 統一發票

Cross-border GPU rental has payment details that comparison posts never mention, but they hit your real cost:

- **International platforms price in USD and charge cards online.** Most Taiwanese banks add a foreign-transaction fee of about 1.5% — a US$100 bill costs roughly NT$47 extra. A card with over 1.5% cashback on foreign spending cancels it out.
- **Local monthly providers quote in TWD and issue 統一發票** — smoothest for corporate expensing, but usually via bank transfer and contracts.
- **Hourly platforms use prepaid balances or card charges**; when the balance runs out, machines stop. No end-of-month bill shock — but check your balance before kicking off a long job.

A useful habit: treat hourly compute like a utility. Top up a fixed budget at the start of the month; if you blow through it, that is your cue to audit which jobs were sitting idle on a running machine.
