### Content Gaps and Opportunities

- **Topic gap:** No search result gives a concrete Taiwan buyer a GMI-versus-Glows.ai choice based on access model, GPU class, billing, and data residency.
- **Depth gap:** Generic GPU-cloud pages do not separate a one-person/SMB self-serve workflow from a committed enterprise deployment.
- **Quality gap:** Taiwanese location and compliance claims are easy to overstate. Require current location, certification, and product-availability sources before publication.
