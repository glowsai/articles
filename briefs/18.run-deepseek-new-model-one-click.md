### Content Gaps and Opportunities

- **Topic gap:** Nobody converts VRAM tiers into hourly rental dollars. "Q4 Flash = 96 GB = $1.68/hour" appears nowhere in the SERP.
- **Topic gap:** No page gives the honest decision tree: V4 Flash quantized vs. V4 Pro via API vs. R1-Distill on a single 24 GB card for budget users.
- **Depth gap:** Competitors state VRAM minimums but not the rent-vs-buy break-even (hours) for each tier.
- **Quality gap:** Several pages conflate "13B active parameters" with "13B of VRAM" — clarifying that full weights must be resident is a trust win (knightli gets this right; most don't).
