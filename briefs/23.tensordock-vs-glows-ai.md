### Content Gaps and Opportunities

- **Topic gap:** There is no direct TensorDock–Glows.ai decision page for a developer who cares about both per-second billing and predictable environment setup.
- **Depth gap:** Alternatives pages mention availability but do not provide a pre-rental checklist for a host marketplace.
- **Quality gap:** Availability reports change quickly; the article needs a dated, reproducible availability check—not an unqualified reliability claim.
