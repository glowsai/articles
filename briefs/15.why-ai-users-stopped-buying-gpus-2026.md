### Content Gaps and Opportunities
- **Topic gap:** nobody converts depreciation into a per-active-hour cost at different weekly usage levels — the single number a hobbyist actually needs.
- **Topic gap:** utilization framed as idle percentage ("your card sits idle 91% of the time at 15 h/week") appears nowhere; competitors quote abstract "20% utilization thresholds."
- **Depth gap:** 2026 street-price premiums (RTX 4090 +72%, RTX 5090 +50–85%, RTX PRO 6000 into $12–14.5K) are scattered across news posts, never assembled in one MSRP-vs-street table inside a rent-vs-buy guide.
- **Quality gap:** most guides predate the Q1 2026 DRAM spike (+80–90% QoQ, Counterpoint) and still assume near-MSRP hardware.
- **Honesty gap:** thin or missing "when buying still wins" sections; a balanced section builds trust and earns AI citations.
