## Content Brief: free AI image generator limits

### Search Intent
Commercial-informational hybrid. Searchers have hit a wall on a free tool (credits gone, watermark, resolution cap) and are comparing options before deciding whether to pay or switch. Google rewards listicle/comparison formats with tables ("Best free AI image generators 2026", "limits explained" pages). Audience: hobbyists and creators with intermediate technical comfort but no high-end GPU; they know what Midjourney is, may not know what SDXL or Flux is. The winning format: an honest limits comparison table + a concrete escape route with verifiable math.

### Content Gaps and Opportunities
- **Topic gap:** No ranking page frames free tiers as a funnel with a documented "power user exit" (open weights + hourly GPU). All five treat "free tools" as the end state.
- **Topic gap:** None compares per-image cost of a rented RTX 4090 running SDXL/Flux vs Midjourney Basic vs DALL·E 3 API. This math is the information gain.
- **Depth gap:** Watermark and license terms (SDXL CreativeML Open RAIL++-M, Flux.1 schnell Apache 2.0, Flux.1 dev non-commercial) covered nowhere in the top 5.
- **Quality gap:** Competitor limit numbers are frequently stale; we date-stamp every figure (verified July 2026) and link primary sources (Midjourney docs, Leonardo pricing, Adobe plans).
- Gap priority: cost-math section = high impact × high advantage ÷ low effort → lead with it after the comparison table.
