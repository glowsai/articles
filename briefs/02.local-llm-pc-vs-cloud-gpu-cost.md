### Content Gaps and Opportunities

- **Topic gap:** Nobody prices the *full* build at July 2026 street prices — the DDR5 spike (3-4x since mid-2025) and NAND/SSD spike (drives doubled/tripled vs late 2024) change the answer, and most ranking pages still quote 2024-25 component prices.
- **Topic gap:** Break-even expressed in *hours per week of real usage*, not 24/7 datacenter utilization — hobbyists don't run 24/7.
- **Depth gap:** Electricity treated as an afterthought; we compute $/hr from EIA 2026 rates and a measured system draw.
- **Quality gap:** Most pieces are one-sided (pro-cloud or pro-local). An honest "when buying wins" section is rare and builds trust.
- **Depth gap:** Idle power cost of an always-on tower is almost never counted.
