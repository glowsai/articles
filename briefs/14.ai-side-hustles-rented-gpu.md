## Content Gaps and Opportunities

- **Topic gap:** Nobody frames startup cost as GPU-hours. Every competitor assumes $20–99/month SaaS subscriptions as cost of goods. A rented-GPU cost-of-goods table (deliverable cost in cents) is absent from the SERP.
- **Topic gap:** No competitor covers self-hosted open-weight tools (ComfyUI/Flux, BreezyVoice, Wan 2.2, LoRA fine-tuning) as the production stack — they all recommend Midjourney/ElevenLabs/Canva subscriptions that eat margin.
- **Depth gap:** Earnings claims are either hype ($1,000/month blueprints) or vague. Sourced market-rate data per hustle (Fiverr gig prices, Upwork hourly ranges, POD per-sale margins, Shorts RPM) is thin everywhere.
- **Quality gap:** Almost no citations. Sourced numbers with dates are an immediate E-E-A-T win.

Gap priority: unit-economics table (high impact × high advantage ÷ low effort — we already have sibling cost-math posts to build on).
