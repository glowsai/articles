# SEO Brief 07 — turn-photo-into-video-ai-10-minutes (new page mode)

## Content Brief: turn photo into video AI

### Search Intent
Informational with a transactional edge: the searcher has a still photo and wants it animated, today, ideally free or cheap. Google rewards two SERP formats for this query: (1) hosted-tool landing pages (Canva, Adobe Firefly, Vidu, VEED) and (2) hands-on tutorials for open models (Wan 2.2 + ComfyUI). Audience: hobbyists and creators, low-to-mid technical level, most without a 24 GB GPU.

### Content Gaps and Opportunities
- **Topic gap:** nobody bridges "I have no GPU" → open model. Tool pages hide costs behind credits; tutorials assume owned hardware.
- **Depth gap:** zero pages publish per-clip cost math (GPU $/hr × render minutes). That table is our information gain.
- **Quality gap:** every "in minutes" claim is unaudited. An honest minute-by-minute timeline (including the one-time model download most tutorials gloss over) builds trust.
