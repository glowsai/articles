### Content Gaps and Opportunities

- **Topic gap:** No result explains when a marketplace’s lower 4090 rate is worth choosing and when a repeatable, managed environment is the lower-risk choice.
- **Depth gap:** Existing comparisons separate price and reliability but do not price a checkpointable batch job against an interactive workflow that must be restarted quickly.
- **Quality gap:** Marketplace listings fluctuate. Make the article date-stamped, identify the listing configuration, and avoid presenting a marketplace low as a guaranteed price.
- **Information gain:** A two-scenario “cheap GPU” worksheet: one interruptible batch run and one interactive ComfyUI/VS Code session, with restart, storage, and location assumptions shown.
