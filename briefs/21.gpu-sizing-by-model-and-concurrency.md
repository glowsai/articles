# Content Brief: GPU Sizing for an Open-Weight Model at a Target Concurrency

**Status:** Backlog. Do not draft until a model and benchmark configuration are approved.

**ICP:** AI builders and MLOps teams selecting inference capacity.

**Search intent:** Implementation and consideration - "how many GPUs do I need for [model]" and "[model] inference concurrency".

**Working title:** How Many GPUs Do You Need to Serve `<model>` at `<concurrency>` Concurrent Requests?

**Required evidence:** exact model/version, quantization, context length, framework version, GPU and region, measured throughput, p50/p95 latency, concurrency, benchmark commands, and date.

**Outline:** answer-first sizing table; workload assumptions; benchmark method; results; capacity recommendation by concurrency tier; Glows.ai deployment path; limitations and update date.

**Guardrail:** Never infer throughput from VRAM alone or promise a GPU count without a measured workload.
