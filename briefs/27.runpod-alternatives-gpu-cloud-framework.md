### Content Gaps and Opportunities

- **Topic gap:** No page is explicitly designed for Taiwan/SEA developers who need both a hardware comparison and locality/data-residency questions.
- **Depth gap:** Lists name providers but rarely force a reader to answer the nine questions that determine actual cost and fit.
- **Information gain:** A reusable nine-question scorecard with links to the direct RunPod, Vast.ai, TensorDock, GMI Cloud, and Lambda comparison spokes.
