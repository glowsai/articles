What are the rules, and what are our options if it can't?" Knowledge level: business-literate, not lawyers — they want the rules named, dated, and linked, then a practical answer.

## Content Gaps and Opportunities

- **Topic gap:** No ranking page names the three actual sector rules (個資法 §21, 金管會委外辦法, 衛福部電子病歷辦法) in one place with dates and links. High impact, low effort → priority 1.
- **Topic gap:** Nobody covers the Nov 2025 個資法修正 (PDPC, breach notification) and Dec 2025 AI 基本法 as the reason this question is urgent *now*.
- **Depth gap:** "資料落地台灣" is asserted by vendors but never operationalized — what actually makes an AI deployment "in-country" (instance location + storage + no overseas API calls)?
- **Quality gap:** No page gives real hourly GPU pricing for the in-Taiwan option; competitors say "contact sales." Concrete NT$/hr numbers are our differentiator.
- **Format gap:** No comparison table of deployment options (overseas SaaS API vs overseas GPU cloud vs Taiwan GPU cloud vs on-prem). FS/AI-citation target.
